About Me

Hi, I'm Samurai Owl. I am a God-fearing musician, gamer, and storyteller.

I love to explore other worlds, create new ones, and describe them to others, whether through word or song.

In my mind, natural and industrial are most beautiful in tandem, and my music reflects that. I love to use synths alongside sampled instruments to create epic cinematic soundscores, or high energy dubsteppish stuff.
